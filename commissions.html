After doing web design as a hobby for three years, I realized that it was time I started helping other people with their website too.
I've been in a race between me and some friends to become the best out of our group, and currently I'm stuck right in the middle by a bit. In doing this, I hope I can further my experience in creation/design and learn a couple new things.
An HTML/CSS outline without JS would be $15.
A full website with all three would be $20.
Before beginning the project, all I need is a basic idea/concept of how you want the site to look, and I'll imagine the rest for you, while updating and asking you questions along the way.
If you aren't satisfied with my work or need to cancel before the project is finished, I'll put the price down to just 50% of the original.
